# Businesses Over $10M Turnover: North Melbourne

Focused analysis of businesses with annual turnover exceeding $10 million across 7 northern Melbourne LGAs.
Source: ABS CABEE (Jul 2021 - Jun 2025), SEIFA 2021.

These are enterprise-scale businesses - the largest employers, highest spenders, and most complex operations in the region.
