# Businesses Over $200K Turnover: North Melbourne

Focused analysis of businesses with annual turnover exceeding $200,000 across 7 northern Melbourne LGAs.
Source: ABS CABEE (Jul 2021 - Jun 2025), SEIFA 2021.

These are the businesses with enough revenue to invest in consulting services, technology, and transformation.
