# Northern Melbourne Enterprise AI Playbook — $10M+ Businesses

**Briefing for NORTH Link Business Forum**
Prepared March 2026 | Data: ABS CABEE June 2025, SEIFA 2021

---

## 1. The Enterprise Market at a Glance

The NORTH Link region has **1,493 businesses** turning over more than $10 million per year. These aren't SMEs dabbling with AI — they're organisations with departments, procurement processes, and real transformation budgets.

| Metric | Value |
|--------|------:|
| $10M+ businesses | 1,493 |
| Share of all businesses | 1.4% |
| $5M-$10M (pipeline) | 1,521 |
| Growth 2023-2025 | +5.4% (+77) |
| Businesses with 20+ employees | 2,100 |

The $10M+ tier is **growing faster than the overall business count** — enterprise concentration is increasing in Melbourne's north.

| LGA | 2023 | 2025 | Change | Signal |

| Mitchell | 23 | 26 | +13.0% | Greenfield growth — new enterprises following population |
| Darebin | 251 | 275 | +9.6% | Retail enterprise cluster consolidating |
| Whittlesea | 318 | 335 | +5.3% | Steady growth in the growth corridor |
| Banyule | 126 | 128 | +1.6% | Mature, stable — replacement market |

**The pitch**: *"Nillumbik's enterprise count grew 34% in two years. Merri-Bek grew 18%. These businesses are scaling past $10M and they need enterprise systems — not the spreadsheets that got them to $5M."*
